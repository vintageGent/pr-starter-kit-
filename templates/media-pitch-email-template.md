<!--
  This is a template for a "cold" media pitch email.
  The goal is to convince a journalist you've never spoken to that your story is worth their time.
  Personalization is the #1 key to success.
-->

**Subject: [Choose ONE of these formats]**

*   **Format 1 (Story Idea):** Story Idea: [A short, compelling angle]
*   **Format 2 (Specific to them):** For your [Column/Section Name] section
*   **Format 3 (Direct):** [Your Company Name] launches [Product Name] to solve [Problem]

---

Hi [Journalist's First Name],

<!--
  Start with a personalized sentence. Prove you're not a robot. Show you know who they are.
  This is the most important part of the email.
-->
I'm a long-time reader of your work on [Their Beat/Topic, e.g., consumer tech]. I particularly enjoyed your recent article on [Specific Article Title] and it made me think you'd be interested in a related story.

<!--
  Now, the pitch. Keep it to 2-3 short paragraphs.
  Paragraph 1: Get straight to the point. What's the news and why should their audience care?
-->
I'm writing to you today because [Your Company Name] is launching [Product/Service Name] on [Date]. It's a [brief product description] designed to help [Target Audience] solve [Problem] by [Unique Solution].

<!--
  Paragraph 2: Give them the most interesting hook. A surprising data point, a powerful customer story, or a bold vision.
-->
What makes this different is [Your Key Differentiator or most interesting fact]. For example, our beta testers found that it [mention a compelling statistic, e.g., "reduced their weekly planning time by 50%"].

<!--
  Use bullet points for skimmable facts if you have them.
-->
*   **Key Feature 1:** [Benefit]
*   **Key Feature 2:** [Benefit]
*   **Key Feature 3:** [Benefit]

<!--
  The Call to Action. Make it easy for them to say yes.
-->
I've included the full press release below for more details and have a full press kit with high-res images available. Would you be open to a brief 15-minute demo next week?

Best regards,

[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Your Company]
[Link to your Website]
[Link to your LinkedIn/Twitter (Optional)]
